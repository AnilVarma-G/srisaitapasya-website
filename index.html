Festival Wise Celebrations

At school, celebrate festivals to engage children in cultural experiences. For Diwali, have rangoli making and diya painting. During Christmas, decorate trees, do Secret Santa, sing carols, and organize charity drives. For Chinese New Year, make dragon crafts, practice calligraphy, create lanterns, and sample Chinese food. Holi can involve a color run, water balloon battles, themed art, and traditional sweets. Eid al-Fitr celebrations may include crafts, henna designs, a feast, and storytelling about the festival's significance.
